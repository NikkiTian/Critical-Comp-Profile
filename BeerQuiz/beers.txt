Pale Lager
- Heineken
- Budweiser
- Estrella
Light, crisp, and super easy to drink. Great for hot days or just chilling.

Pilsner
- San Miguel
- Stella Artois
- Carlsberg 
Still crisp like a lager, but with more flavor and a little hop kick.

IPA (India Pale Ale)
- Goose Island IPA
- lagunitas
- New Belgium Voodoo Ranger
Bold and hoppy with a punch of bitterness. Often citrusy, piney, or tropical.

Pale Ale
- Sierra Nevada Pale Ale
- Dales
- Coopers
Easygoing but flavorful, with just the right touch of hops to keep things interesting.

Wheat
- Hoegaarden
- Vedett
- Blue Moon
Smooth and hazy, often with hints of citrus or spice. Super refreshing.

Stout
- Guinness
- Left Hand Milk Stout
- Hardywood Park Gingerbread Stout
Dark, rich, and roasty. Think coffee, chocolate, or dessert vibes.

Sour
- Crooked Stave Sour Rosé
- Revolution Freedom of Press
- Sierra Nevada Wild Little Thing
Tart and tangy with a fruity twist. Not your average beer—fun and unexpected.

Saison
- Brooklyn Sorachi Ace
- Boulevard Tank 7
- Saison Dupont
Dry, slightly spicy, and full of character. A bit wild in a good way.
